Topic models¶

These are the topic models used for my sentiment analysis, using gensim's implementation of Latent Dirichlet Allocation (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latent_Dirichlet_allocation). The topic models were trained on approximately 500,000 user comments on Kickstarter technology projects. For this model, 30 topics were used.

Mouse over a topic circle on the left, and on the right you will see the most relevant words for the topic. For example, topic 17 contains words like "refund", "money", "scam", clearly indicating a topic where backers are very unhappy. By contrast, topic 15 is very positive, and relates to optimism at the beginning of a successfully-funded project. Notice that the bigram "cant_wait" is very popular here, whereas "wait" by itself is not common in this topic.
